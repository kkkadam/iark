GUIDELINES FOR COMPLETION OF THIS FORM

The following guidelines will help Old Mutual Group Assurance to process your claim quickly and accurately

  • These claim forms must be completed by an authorised representative of the employer.
  • Complete the application form fully and in detail as it gives us important information.
  • Write your answers in clear black or blue block letters so that it is easy to read.
  • If the form is completed electronically, please print, sign, stamp and scan the form to send to us.
